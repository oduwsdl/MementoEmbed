Web archives preserve versions of web pages. These archived web pages are known as mementos. Mementos have additional information, like the date and time they were captured, which archive they belong to, and what other versions are available. This information is not typically provided in existing embeds produced for blogs and other web pages. Mementos are identified with a special URL known as a URI-M. Here are some examples of URI-Ms:

  • From the Internet Archive: http://web.archive.org/web/20180621081452/http://ws-dl.blogspot.com/
  • From Archive.today: http://archive.is/YZ6OH
  • From Arquivo.pt, the Portuguese Web Archive: http://arquivo.pt/wayback/20000823154833/http://www.parquedasnacoes.pt/pt/expo98/
